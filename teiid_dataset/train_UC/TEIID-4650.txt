SybaseIQ translator: week function can't be pushed directly
SybaseIQ translator pushes WEEK function calls directly to SAP IQ. SAP IQ doesn't support such function. See [Date and Time Functions docs|http://help.sap.com/saphelp_iq1611_iqrefbb/helpdata/en/a5/2b07be84f21015baa5a63dd21b850b/content.htm?frameset=/en/a5/3f6efb84f21015af0e8594ce5cd68e/frameset.htm&current_toc=/en/a8/911fe984f21015a3e3f2c342c4e05e/plain.htm&node_id=115&show_children=false]

Query:
{code:sql}
SELECT WEEK(dateValue) FROM BQT1.SMALLA;
{code}
Pushed source query:
{code:sql}
[SELECT week(g_0."datevalue") FROM "bqt-server"."dvqe"."SmallA" AS g_0]
{code}
